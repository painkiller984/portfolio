Privacy policy

Last updated: January 10, 2026

We respect your personal data and only collect what we need to run the Warm Heart shop, deliver your order, and keep in touch if you ask us to.

Data we collect

  • Account and order details: name, contact data, delivery address.
  • Payment information processed through secure providers. We do not store full card numbers or CVV codes.
  • Browsing data: pages viewed, device and browser information, and cookie data to keep your cart and improve navigation.
  • Messages you send us in support or feedback forms.

How we use it

  • Process and deliver orders, handle returns, and notify you about status updates.
  • Provide support and answer questions you send us.
  • Share updates about new plaids and offers if you subscribe. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Improve site performance, prevent fraud, and keep the store secure.

Cookies and analytics

We use cookies to remember items in your cart, save language or currency preferences, and understand how the shop performs. You can adjust browser settings to limit cookies, but some features may not work without them.

Sharing

We share data only with trusted partners that help operate the shop: payment processors, delivery services, and analytics providers. We may also share data if required by law. We do not sell personal data.

Storage and security

We keep data only as long as needed for orders, legal obligations, or your account history. We use encryption, access controls, and monitoring to protect it, while understanding that no online system can be 100% secure.

Your choices

  • Request a copy, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails through the link inside each message or by contacting us directly.
  • Manage cookies in your browser settings. Essential cookies are required for checkout to work correctly.

Questions or requests

Contact us and we will help with any privacy questions or data requests related to Warm Heart.
