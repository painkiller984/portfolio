About Warm Heart

Warm Heart is a shop for soft plaids that bring calm tones and tactile warmth to everyday rooms. We combine durable weaving with gentle finishes so the blanket you buy today stays cozy season after season.

Natural-feel fibers Muted, easy-to-match palette Carefully packed for gifting
Shop plaids

Every plaid is brushed for softness, checked for shedding, and shipped in recyclable wrapping.

Comfort, tested

We try every batch ourselves to be sure it drapes softly and keeps you warm without weight.

Thoughtful design

Calm beige, sand, and graphite tones pair with simple geometric patterns, so a plaid fits any sofa or bedroom.

Comfort first

Double-brushed surfaces make the fabric gentle on skin, while balanced weight keeps warmth without feeling heavy.

Made to last

Dense weaving helps plaids hold their shape, and color-stable dyes keep tones soft even after many washes.

Caring service

We help pick the right size and tone, arrange quick exchanges if something is off, and stay in touch until you are comfortable.

What makes our plaids feel different

We focus on tactile details you notice the first evening: a brushed surface, neat edging, and balanced weight that stays warm yet breathable.

  • Four core sizes (110x170 to 150x200 cm) to cover both armchairs and beds.
  • Natural and blended fibers for softness with easy care and color stability.
  • Edge reinforcement that helps plaids keep their shape after frequent washing.
  • Each plaid photographed in daylight so the color you choose looks the same at home.

Responsible approach

We choose mills that can trace their yarns, avoid harsh finishes, and help us keep a stable palette for reorders.

  • Recyclable sleeves and minimal plastic.
  • Short production runs to reduce overstock.
  • Transparent pricing without seasonal gimmicks.

Need help choosing?

Tell us about your interior palette or who you are buying for. We will suggest colors, sizes, and care tips that match.
